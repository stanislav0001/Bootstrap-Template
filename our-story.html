OUR STORY

noun, a reward or recompense.

We are a team of app marketing enthusiasts who believe that app and game developers can use rewards to create a unique and engaging experience for users.

The CashMyApps product was launched in December 2011 under the app marketing company Fiksu. Five years later, it was spun out and became part of the broader Meed Rewards Platform.

Today, Meed helps thousands of brands (Amazon, Zynga, and Coca Cola to name a few) acquire and engage its users, leveraging an audience of over 1.6 million monthly active users.

Meed is headquartered in Boston with offices in San Francisco, New York City and London.
